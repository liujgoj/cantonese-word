**粵語羅馬字溜歌方案**（_Liujgoj Romanization System for Cantonese_）係一套基於粵語羅馬字嘅溜歌式書寫方案，簡稱「溜歌羅馬字」。

「溜歌羅馬字」具有以下特點：

**1. 標點與書寫規則**  
「溜歌羅馬字」採用英文嘅標點符號同書寫規則。

**2. 以單詞為書寫單位**  
「溜歌羅馬字」以羅馬字母拼寫，以「單詞」作為基本書寫單位，單詞之間用空格分開。  
例如：

> _Gamjyath ge tinjhei hour hour_（今日嘅天氣好好）

# ✅ English Translation

**Liujgoj Romanization System for Cantonese** is a Liujgoj-based Romanization system designed for fluent (“liugou-style”) writing of Cantonese. It is abbreviated as **“Liujgoj Romanization.”**

Liujgoj Romanization has the following features:

**1. Punctuation and writing conventions**  
Liujgoj Romanization adopts English punctuation and writing conventions.

**2. Word-based writing system**  
Liujgoj Romanization uses the Roman alphabet and takes the _word_ as the basic writing unit. Words are separated by spaces.  
Example:

> _Gamjyath ge tinjhei hour hour_ (“今日嘅天氣好好” / The weather today is very good.)

**3. Right-oriented syllable maximization principle**  
A word may consist of one or more syllables. Syllables are segmented from right to left.

- If a hyphen “-” is present, segmentation must occur at the hyphen.
    
- If there is no hyphen, segmentation follows the principle of _maximizing legal syllables_.
    

For example, the word **hamhbaanghlaangh** can be segmented into three syllables:

> hamh · baangh · laangh

For example, the word “粵語” is written in Liujgoj Romanization as:

> **Yvthyvq**
